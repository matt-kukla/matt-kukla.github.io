I am a third-year undergraduate mathematics student at the University of Maryland. My mathematical interests revolve broadly around category theory, algebraic geometry (primarily derived algebraic geometry, p-adic Hodge theory), and logic. Sometimes I dabble in computer science as well, usually within topics related to formal verification, type theory, and scientific computing.

I'm also a researcher at The Math Citadel, an independent mathematics research firm.
