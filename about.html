About



I'm a mathematics student who enjoys approaching things from the n-point of view.  But I have many interests outside of mathematics too. I'm an avid listener of music (mainly classical, Russian/Yugoslavian folk, klezmer, and bits of jazz), and I occasionally play the piano.  I've been known to indulge in philosophy as well.  Sometimes I spout absurd comments and bits of mathematics on Twitter.

As much as I enjoy inhaling abstract nonsense, sometimes I take a few steps down from the tower of abstraction to indulge in something a bit more concrete such as programming, small electronics projects (many regarding radio/antenna theory), collecting vintage computing equipment, and befriending the local birds.

Currently I am a second-year undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, where I am working towards degrees in mathematics and computer science.
