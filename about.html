I am an undergraduate mathematics student at the University of Maryland and a researcher at The Math Citadel, an independent mathematics research firm.

My research interests primarily revolve around topics in homotopy theory, algebraic geometry, and mathematical logic, as held together by the common thread of category theory. Occasionally I dabble in computer science (usually within topics related to scientific computing, formal verification, type theory, and systems programming) as well.
