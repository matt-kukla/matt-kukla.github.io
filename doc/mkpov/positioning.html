positioning.inc

Various macros for positioning objects.

For demonstration purposes, take S to be a sphere primitive with radius 0.5.

N_roots(N, R, Ob):

Constructs a union containing copies of object Ob at each Nth root of unity in the xz-plane, scaled by R.

Row(Ob, Start, N, Space):

Constructs a union containing N copies of object Ob in a row, starting at point Start and spaced apart by vector Space.
