I am a third-year undergraduate mathematics student at the University of Maryland. My primary mathematical interests very broadly revolve around higher category theory, (derived) algebraic geometry, and logic. Sometimes I dabble in computer science; my interests here could probably be described as some odd union of formally verified functional tripe, scientific computing, and "brutalist" Unix/DOS systems programming.

Currently I'm also a researcher at The Math Citadel, an independent mathematics research firm.
